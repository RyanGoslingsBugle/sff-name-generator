THE SF/F NAME GENERATOR

Ever wanted to be a top-selling Science Fiction/Fantasy author with a string of awards and the adoration of awkward teenage nerds worldwide? Now, you too can enjoy the feeling of Internet forums clamouring for your blood for writing a nice story about spaceships or whatever!

Just push the button and find the title and inspiration for your next adventure, preferably not starring a straight white guy named Dirk Stone with weapon skills and a tortured past.

You might see odd chunks of some favourite titles pop up now and again; I assure you, I have as much love and respect for them as anyone. It's all in good fun.
